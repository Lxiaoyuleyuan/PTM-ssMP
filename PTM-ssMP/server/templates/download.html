Dataset description:

    The experimentally determined phosphorylation, lysine acetylation, ubiquitination, sumoylation, methylation, sulfation, proteolytic cleavage, O-GalNAc and O-GlcNAc site are extracted from several public database including HPRD (Peri, et al., 2004), dbPTM version 3.0 (Lu, et al., 2012), Phospho.ELM (Diella, et al., 2004), PhosphoSitePlus (Hornbeck, et al., 2011), dbOGAP (Wang, et al., 2011) and SysPTM (Li, et al., 2009). We extract all of the experimentally verified PTM sites in human protein from these databases for further analysis. To eliminate sequence redundancy and avoid overestimates of the prediction performance, we use CD-HIT (Huang, et al., 2010) to ensure that none of the protein sequences show a sequence similarity of more than 40% for each PTM type. The details of the final dataset are shown in the following table:

Ubi,ubiquination; Ace, acetylation; Meth, methylation; Sumo, sumoylation; O-GalNAc, O-N-acetylgalactosamine; O-GlcNAc, O-N-acetylglucosamine; Prot, proteolytic cleavage
